About

I started learning web develpment in highschool, and have always had a love for technology. I build my own computers as a hobby and I've worked at call centers, providing technical support for HP, XBOX, and Verizon. I also worked for Comcast as a Cable Technician out in the field.

My family and friends have come to me often for technical advice and problem solving. I also recently did a training at my local church congregation, covering the features of the 'Gospel Library' App and how to use them on varying brands and types of devices.

I love being apart of Lambda School and the experience I am gaining there. I am striving to be a valuable part of the Lambda Community by working with and helping other students. I am pushing myself to complete of the Stretch Goals that are a part of their curriculum.
